Mil Nicholson, our reader, produces audiobooks from her studio in the quiet Appalachian Mountains. Her range of voices includes both male and female of all ages, specializing in the accents of the British Isles. She has been acclaimed in particular for her rendering of the many voices in the novels of Charles Dickens, and for breathing life into his sometimes long monologues.
