What Format Are the Texts In?

All texts are available in PDF form. The downloadable files are linked in brackets next to the full-text or segment title.

Selected texts are available as audio books. These files are also downloadable.

Starting March, 2019, we have begun digitizing all works into the e-book formats of epub (e-readers, tablets, cell phones) and Mobi (for Kindle). These files are also downloadable. Epub and Mobi files will generally not be broken into segments.
